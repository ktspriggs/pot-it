How to Shop

Picking the right plant for your space can be difficult, but we're here to help. Whether your home has minimal light or an abundance, there's a perfect plant for you. We've provided care instructions for all plants so that you can select ones that will thrive and bring joy to your home. Welcome to our plant family, and happy shopping!

New Arrivals

Didn't find what you were looking for? New plants arrive weekly so that we can provide you a wide range of selection. To stay updated on new arrivals be sure to subscribe to our newsletter, and contact us on social media for any plant requests.

Care

About

We are decicated to spreading the joy of plants by providing a variety of products that are sure to fit various lifestyles and budgets. New plant parents will gain confidence as we continue to inspire people with one plant at a time.
